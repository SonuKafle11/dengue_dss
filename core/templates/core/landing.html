Dengue Decision Support System

A simple tool to help you understand your dengue risk early.

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection common in tropical countries. Early detection of warning signs can save lives. This system lets you check your symptoms quickly, get a clinical risk score, and decide whether you need to see a doctor.
