Start Symptom Check
How it works

Four steps from symptoms to result

The whole process takes under two minutes. No account is needed for the initial check.

Answer 14 questions

You are shown a list of standard dengue warning signs. Select which symptoms apply to you. The form takes about 60 seconds to fill in.

Get an instant score

Each symptom carries a clinical weight. The system adds them up and places you into one of two risk bands: Low Risk, or High Risk.

ML cross-validation

A trained Hybrid Naive Bayes model analyzes NS1, IgG, IgM, Platelet Count, and WBC Count to predict Dengue or No Dengue and provides a confidence score.

Doctor follow-up if needed

High-risk results are queued for a doctor to review. You will be prompted to register so the doctor can access your record and add lab results.

Access levels

What you can do without logging in

Some features are open to everyone. Others require an account so that your records can be stored and reviewed safely.

No account required

Public symptom check

You can check your symptoms and receive a risk result at any time without registering. Nothing is saved to the database until you choose to create an account.

Patient account

Saved history & doctor access

Registering lets you store your results, view past checks, and have a doctor review any high-risk submissions. Registration is free and takes under a minute.

Doctor account

Case review & prescriptions

Doctors log in to review patient records, enter laboratory values, predict Dengue or No Dengue using the trained ML model, and generate dosage recommendations.

Understanding results

What the three bands mean

Low Risk means the patient's symptoms are less likely to indicate dengue. High Risk means immediate medical assessment is recommended.

Ready to check your symptoms?

The check takes about a minute. No account needed to get started — you can register afterwards if you want to save your result.
