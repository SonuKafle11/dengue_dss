About Us

This Dengue Decision Support System is built as part of an academic project to support the early detection and management of dengue fever. Our aim is to provide patients and doctors with a simple, reliable tool that combines clinical scoring with machine learning prediction.

What this system does

  • Allows patients to check symptoms without logging in.
  • Calculates a clinical risk score based on 13 standard dengue symptoms.
  • Classifies the result as Low Risk, Possible Dengue, or High Risk.
  • For high-risk cases, doctors review the case and provide medication dosage based on BMI.
  • Uses a Naive Bayes machine learning model trained on patient data.

Who can use this system

  • Patients — to check symptoms and view their reports.
  • Doctors — to review patients, enter lab results, and recommend treatment.
  • Admins — to manage users and monitor the system.
