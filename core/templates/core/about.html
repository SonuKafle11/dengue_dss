About This System

This system was developed as a final-year project to help identify dengue risk at an early stage. It combines symptom-based scoring with a trained machine learning model to support faster and more accurate clinical decision-making.

Background

Why this was built

Diagnosing dengue in its early stage can be challenging because many symptoms overlap with other diseases. This project was developed to support healthcare professionals by providing a fast, consistent, and reliable dengue risk assessment and dosage recommendations.

System capabilities

What the system actually does

The workflow runs from a public symptom check all the way through to a doctor-confirmed dosage recommendation.

Public symptom check

Visitors can submit 14 clinical symptoms without registering. No account, no personal data stored — just a quick risk check.

Scoring

Each symptom is assigned a weighted score. The total score classifies the patient into Low Risk or High Risk.

Machine learning cross-check

A trained Hybrid Naive Bayes model analyzes NS1, IgG, IgM, Platelet Count, and WBC Count to predict dengue status with a confidence score.

Doctor review queue

After reviewing the patient's symptoms, the doctor enters lab values. The system predicts dengue status and provides clinical recommendations.

Weight-based dosage

Paracetamol dosage is calculated using age and weight. IV fluid requirements are estimated using the Holliday–Segar method.

Patient history

Patients can review previous assessments, laboratory results, and dengue prediction history anytime after logging in.

User roles

Three roles, one platform

Access is scoped by role. Each user type sees only what they need.

Patient

Check & track your records

Complete symptom assessments, view dengue risk results, access previous assessment history, and review laboratory results and dosage recommendations.

Doctor

Review flagged cases

See all patients in your queue, enter lab results, and generate a dosage plan.

Admin

Manage users & data

Manage patient and doctor accounts, maintain user records, and oversee the system database through the administrator dashboard.
