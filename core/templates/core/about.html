About This System

Built as a final-year academic project, this system combines a standard clinical scoring rubric with a trained machine learning model to help identify dengue risk early — before a patient deteriorates.

Background

Why this was built

Dengue cases are often missed or misclassified in early stages because warning signs overlap with other febrile illnesses. Nurses and junior doctors sometimes have limited time to apply the full WHO scoring checklist. This system was built to make that triage step faster and more consistent.

System capabilities

What the system actually does

The workflow runs from a public symptom check all the way through to a doctor-confirmed dosage recommendation.

Public symptom check

Visitors can submit 13 clinical symptoms without registering. No account, no personal data stored — just a quick risk check.

Clinical scoring

Each symptom carries a weighted score. The total maps to one of three bands: Low Risk, Possible Dengue, or High Risk.

Machine learning cross-check

A Naive Bayes classifier trained on a labelled patient dataset runs in parallel and flags cases where the ML output diverges from the rule-based score.

Doctor review queue

High-risk records are automatically queued for a doctor. The doctor can enter NS1 antigen and platelet values before confirming or overriding the classification.

Weight-based dosage

Once a case is confirmed, the dosage engine calculates paracetamol and IV fluid recommendations using the patient's weight and BMI.

Patient history

Registered patients can log in to view all previous checks, lab values, and any notes left by their reviewing doctor.

User roles

Three roles, one platform

Access is scoped by role. Each user type sees only what they need — nothing more.

Patient

Check & track your records

Submit a symptom check, view your risk result, and access the full history of your past submissions and any doctor-added notes.

Doctor

Review flagged cases

See all high-risk patients assigned to your queue, enter lab results, and generate a dosage plan. You can also override the ML classification.

Admin

Manage users & data

Create and deactivate doctor and patient accounts, monitor submission activity, and keep the user directory up to date.
